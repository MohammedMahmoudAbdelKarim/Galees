About GALEES

GALEES is a service that helps you to find and order a babysitter for your children or elderly sitter for old people. You can search for a babysitter with all requirements that fit your needs by filtering profiles, get in contact and plan an appointment. Find a job as a sitter by creating a profile with your experience, specialties and contact info.
